SENDING HUMANITY INTO SPACE IS JUST OUR FIRST STEP

8PACE was founded in 2023 as the world's first creative organization dedicated to developing interstellar travel. Following the successful first manned lunar landing in 2030, 8PACE has ventured beyond Earth and landed on multiple celestial bodies within the solar system. Our goal is to normalize space transportation and make it accessible to everyone at a reasonable price, providing the opportunity for the general public to learn more about space and participate in more space-related activities.
